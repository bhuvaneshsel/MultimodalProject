VOLUNTEERING AT A FOOD BANK

Food Insecurity

Food insecurity is a problem that affects people across the world. People struggling with food insecurity lack access to nutritious foods on a daily basis. This problem can negatively affect the physical and mental health of people if not adressed.

SECOND HARVEST

Second Harvest of Silicon Valley is an organization working to deal with the food insecurity problem. They have many food banks and distribution centers in Silicon Valley. In the distribution centers, they give food to people in need.

WHAT I DID

I volunteered at one of Second Harvest's food bank. I worked on packing food into boxes that passed along a conveyor belt. Other times, I sorted bad food from good food.

IMPACT

By volunteering at a food bank, I was able to help my community as the food that I was packing would be given to people in need. At the end of each volunteer session, they would announce how many pounds of food we packed and I always felt proud of the work I was doing.
